Privacy Policy – Ooink AI Assistant

Last updated: March 2026

This app is an AI assistant for Ooink Ramen restaurant in Seattle, WA. It helps customers learn about the menu while they wait.

Microphone

The microphone is used solely to capture voice questions from customers. Audio is processed in real-time and is not recorded or stored.

Data We Collect

Anonymous conversation logs (questions asked and responses given) are saved to help improve the assistant. No names, contact details, or personal information are collected.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share any data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Firebase and Google Cloud AI services to operate. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on their data practices.
